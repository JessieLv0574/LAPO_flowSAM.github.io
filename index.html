Abstract

We present Adaptable World Models (AWM), a novel framework for robot navigation that enables rapid adaptation to new environments. Unlike traditional approaches that require extensive retraining, our method allows robots to adjust their internal world models with just a few examples. We demonstrate the effectiveness of our approach across multiple challenging environments, showing significant improvements in navigation performance compared to state-of-the-art baselines.

Method Overview

Figure 2: Architecture of our Adaptable World Model framework.

Our approach consists of three main components: (1) a base world model pretrained on diverse environments, (2) an adaptation module that quickly adjusts to new settings, and (3) a planning module that uses the adapted world model for navigation.
